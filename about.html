About Us

The Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Program (AYPP) Alumni Network is a growing, youth-led regional network of graduates of the AYPP who are committed to sustaining peacebuilding action across ASEAN.

Our members are young leaders, professionals, and practitioners working in and with communities affected by conflict, exclusion, and social division. We operate across sectors including civil society, education, governance, and community development while remaining grounded in Rotary's peacebuilding values.

The Alumni Network exists to ensure that peacebuilding does not end with the program, but continues through collaboration, mentorship, and collective action.

Our Story

Celebrating Vietnam

Shaped by perseverance and collective strength, Vietnam's national story is rooted in unity and rebuilding. Vietnamese youth shared this perspective during the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme, highlighting peace through solidarity.

Celebrating Indonesia

Home to hundreds of ethnic groups and languages, Indonesia stands as a living example of unity in diversity. Its youth brought this national principle into the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme, showing how inclusion strengthens peace.

Celebrating Timor-Leste

As one of ASEAN's youngest nations, Timor-Leste carries a powerful legacy of self-determination and reconciliation. Through the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme, its youth shared lessons shaped by healing and resilience.

Celebrating Thailand

Known for cultural grace, respect, and hospitality, Thailand values harmony in everyday life. Thai youth carried these principles into the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme, emphasizing empathy as a foundation for peace.

Celebrating Philippines

A nation shaped by history, struggle, and strong community ties, the Philippines draws strength from bayanihan. This spirit guided Filipino youth participation in the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme.

Celebrating Myanmar

Rich in cultural heritage and spiritual traditions, Myanmar holds a deep emphasis on compassion and humanity. Through the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme, its youth voices reflected resilience, empathy, and hope.

Celebrating Malaysia

Shaped by centuries of cultural exchange, Malaysia's identity is built on coexistence among diverse communities. During the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme, Malaysian youth highlighted dialogue as a daily practice of peace.

Celebrating Lao PDR & Singapore

With a culture grounded in community, humility, and balance, Lao PDR reflects a quieter but deeply rooted approach to peace. Built on multiculturalism and shared responsibility, Singapore reflects how social cohesion is intentionally nurtured. Through the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme, both nations' youth emphasized dialogue, inclusion, and forward-looking peace.

Celebrating Cambodia

From ancient civilizations to modern renewal, Cambodia carries a powerful story of survival and healing. Through the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme, Cambodian youth shared perspectives shaped by resilience, remembrance, and hope.

Celebrating Brunei Darussalam

Known for its strong traditions, social harmony, and deep respect for faith and culture, Brunei reflects how peace is sustained through stability, values, and mutual respect shared by its youth during the Rotary ASEAN Youth Peacebuilders Programme.
