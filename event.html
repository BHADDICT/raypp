Rotary ASEAN Youth Peace Ambassador Peace Talk in Singapore

4th March 2026

Peace felt simple and powerful! Four Rotary ASEAN Peace Ambassadors join the Festival of Peace. A Chinese New Year celebration and Peace Talk organised by the Rotary Club of Peace Builders Singapore.

From the Philippines, Brunei, Laos, and Singapore, it was an evening of stories, shared experiences, and honest reflections about what peace building looks like in our communities across ASEAN.

We were reminded that while our contexts may differ, our commitment to dialogue, understanding, and compassion remains the same.

The 1st RAYPP Alumni Network General Assembly

23rd February 2026

The Rotary ASEAN Youth Peace Builders Alumni Network will convene its General Assembly bringing together alumni representatives from across ASEAN.

The Assembly will serve as an important platform to reaffirm our collective commitment to youth-led peacebuilding, dialogue, and conflict prevention across the region.

Together, we continue advancing a shared vision of a more peaceful and united ASEAN.
